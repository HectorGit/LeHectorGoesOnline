Hector is a musician and recording artist from Guatemala. He helped record location audio for the short film 'Knot For Sale' with Maureen Bradley. He also collaborated with students from Emily Carr School of. Art on the animation 'Piano Girl' by creating sound design, and a musical score.

Hector is experienced in recording techniques, and in the use of multiple Digital Audio Work Stations, such as Logic, Pro Tools and Reaper.
