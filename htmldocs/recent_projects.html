'Piano Girl' - collaboration with Emily Carr School of Art

Hector is collaborating on the short film 'Piano Girl' with Lindsey Ashton and Rina Chang from the Emily Carr School of Art in Vancouver.

The short film is about 'Effy Sharp' - a pianist who desires to play jazz music in spite of her father's opposition.

Hector created the sound design and the musical score for this short film. It will be finished by the 10th of april of 2017.

'Knot For Sale' short film with Maureen Bradley

As part of Maureen Bradley's production workshop in the term of Sept-Dec of 2016, Hector recorded location audio for the short film 'Knot For Sale'.

He also created the musical score for it.

The plot of the short centers on Stacy, a girl scout who can't get anybody to buy her cookies. Stacy decides to try an unorthodox strategy, which enables her to improve her sales.

Audio Recording for '2500 Km' Short film

Hector and his sister Anette collaborated with Daniela Arguello (Twenty Four Productions), on her short film '2500 Km.'

The short film was recorded in Guatemala, during the summer of 2015.

This film tries to depict intermarital abuse, and the hardships of a woman escaping it together with her two childs, in hope of a better life in America.

The short was used as a pitch for a larger series, which would show the story in greater detail. Recently, it has been announced that the series will be on HBO and 'HBO Now'.
